## 🧩 Website Sections

### Hero Section
A concise introduction that presents who I am, what I do, and the technologies I work with. Effective portfolio hero sections should quickly communicate value and include a clear call to action.[page:1]

### About Me
A brief overview of my background, interests, goals, and development journey. This section works like a short visual summary of a CV and helps visitors understand both technical direction and personality.[page:1]

### Skills & Technologies
A categorized showcase of the languages, frameworks, libraries, and tools I use to build scalable and user-friendly applications.

### Projects Showcase
A curated collection of projects including:
- Project descriptions
- Tech stack used

- Live demos

### Experience & Achievements
A timeline-style section highlighting technical growth, coding accomplishments, internships, milestones, and practical exposure.

### Certifications
Recognized certifications that reflect continuous learning, discipline, and upskilling across relevant technical domains.

### Resume
A dedicated resume section for quick recruiter access.

### Contact
Multiple ways to connect with me for professional opportunities, collaboration, and discussions. Portfolio best practices recommend always including clear contact options and profile links.[page:1]
